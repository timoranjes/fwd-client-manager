## üìö Complete Tutorial

### 1. Dashboard Overview

The dashboard shows you at a glance:
- **Total Clients** - Number of clients in system

- **Renewals (30 days)** - Policies expiring soon

**Quick Actions:**

- Click "Search Clients" to find someone

---

### 2. Managing Clients

#### Adding a New Client
1. Click **"Add Client"** on the dashboard
2. Fill in all relevant fields:
   - **Name** (required)
   - **Email** (optional but recommended)
   - **Phone** (optional)
   - **WeChat** (optional but useful for HK clients)
   - **Policy Type** (Life, Health, Critical Illness, etc.)
   - **Coverage Amount** (HKD)
   - **Policy Period** (Start and End dates)
   - **Status** (Active, Expired, Pending, etc.)
3. Click **"Save Client"**

#### Viewing Client Details
1. Click on any client in the list
2. See full details:
   - Contact information
   - Policy details
   - Coverage amount
   - Days until renewal

#### Editing a Client
1. Go to client details
2. Click **"Edit"** button
3. Make changes
4. Click **"Save Client"**

#### Deleting a Client
1. Go to client details
2. Click **"Delete"** button
3. Confirm (cannot be undone)

---

### 3. Tracking Renewals

#### Why Renewals Matter
- Remind clients BEFORE expiration
- Maintain relationships
- Secure new business

#### How to Use Renewals Page
1. Click **"Renewals"** in sidebar
2. Filter by time period:
   - **30 Days** - Expiring within 30 days
   - **60 Days** - Expiring within 60 days
   - **90 Days** - Expiring within 90 days
   - **Expired** - Already expired policies

#### Best Practices
- Check renewals weekly
- Contact clients 30 days before expiration
- Offer review meetings
- Highlight policy benefits

### 5. Activity Logging

Track every client interaction!

#### Types of Activities:
- **Phone Call** - Client calls
- **WeChat** - WeChat messages
- **Email** - Email correspondence
- **Meeting** - In-person meetings
- **Follow-up** - Scheduled follow-ups
- **Renewal** - Renewal discussions
- **Claim** - Claim discussions
- **Other** - Miscellaneous

#### How to Log an Activity:
1. Go to client details
2. Find "Add Activity / Note" section
3. Select activity type
4. Add description
5. Click **"Add"**

#### Why Log Activities?
- Never forget follow-ups
- Track communication history
- Improve client relationships
- Know exactly when you last contacted
